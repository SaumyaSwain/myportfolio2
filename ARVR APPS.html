THE AR RELAY CAR GAME

Description

RELAYCARS: AUGMENTED REALITY APP With over 1 billion AR users expected in 2020, RelayCars is staying ahead of the curve by recently launching an AR version of its popular, virtual showroom experience.
RelayCars AR highlights over 20 curated vehicles from our AR vehicle library with the likes of Audi, BMW, Ford, Lexus, Mercedes, and Toyota among others.

Updates throughout the year will bring you our entire AR vehicle library to mobile devices everywhere.

Simply scan your environment with your mobile device and place your vehicle in it.
You can scale your AR car from tabletop size to full scale. With a click of a button, you can get in the driver seat and get up close and personal with the car’s interior features.

Through the power of augmented reality,you can place your dream car in your driveway, see if it fits in your garage, or share a picture of it to a friend all with the device in your pocket.
